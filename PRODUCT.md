# Product

## Register

product

## Users

This interface is used by a student researcher preparing a production-internship analysis report and by instructors or reviewers who need to inspect the evidence behind the conclusions. They use it in a desktop browser during analysis, revision, and presentation, with mobile support for review and reading.

## Product Purpose

The product visualizes crawler-based text analysis for the study of how college students use generative AI as an intermediary in interpersonal communication. Success means the interface makes the dataset structure, post-comment relationships, scene labels, risk boundaries, platform differences, and representative cases easy to explain from real evidence.

## Brand Personality

Analytical, restrained, investigative. The interface should feel like a research intelligence cockpit rather than a generic admin panel or a decorative AI landing page.

## Anti-references

Avoid ordinary backend templates, generic card grids, random AI-purple gradients, cheap cyberpunk neon, overdecorated glassmorphism, chart dumping without narrative, and interfaces that mix posts and comments as unrelated text.

## Design Principles

1. Start from the research question, not from chart types.
2. Keep posts and comments structurally linked so discussion dynamics remain visible.
3. Use motion to reveal hierarchy and state changes, never to distract from reading.
4. Make each section answer one concrete analytical question.
5. Favor trustworthy density: rich enough for analysis, restrained enough for presentation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, clear keyboard focus, readable Chinese typography, reduced-motion support, and mobile layouts that preserve reading order without horizontal overflow.
